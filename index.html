Hockey: The Greatest Sport on Ice

Hockey is one of the fastest and most exciting sports in the world. The combination of speed, skill, physicality, and nonstop action creates a level of intensity few sports can match.

Basic Rules of Hockey

Below is a quick breakdown of how the game works.

  1. Game Structure
    • Three 20-minute periods
    • Teams of six on the ice
    • Score by getting the puck in the net
  2. Important Gameplay Rules
    • Offside: You can't enter the offensive zone before the puck.
    • Icing: You cannot shoot the puck across both red lines.
    • Faceoffs restart play after a whistle.
  3. Penalties
    • Minor Penalties (2 minutes)
      1. Tripping
      2. Hooking
      3. Slashing
      4. High-sticking
    • Major Penalties (5 minutes)
      1. Fighting
      2. Dangerous hits
      3. Charging
